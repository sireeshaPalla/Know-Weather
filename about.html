Features

Home Page Overview

The homepage of Know Weather provides a clean and user-friendly interface, offering a brief summary of the current weather conditions across major Indian cities. Users can quickly view the temperature, minimum and maximum temperatures, humidity, cloud percentage, sunrise, sunset, and "feels-like" temperature at a glance.

User-friendly Design

The design of Know Weather focuses on providing an intuitive and responsive user experience. The website is accessible on both desktop and mobile devices, ensuring users can check the weather on the go.

City Search Functionality

On the homepage, users will discover a search bar where they can input the name of any city to inquire about its weather. With a single click, detailed weather information for the specified city becomes instantly accessible.
